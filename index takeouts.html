My name is Kaysie Yu and I am 16 years old. I live in San Francisco, California and I am currently a rising junior at International High School.My favorite school subjects are Physics and Math. Furthermore, outside of school I am a competitive figure skater and I am very involved within the San Francisco community. I am one my the chairs on my school's Community Service Steering Committee, I volunteer weekly at Curry Without Worry, a non-profit that serves Nepalese food to San Francisco's homeless population, and started a skating program, in which mentally and physically disabled Chinese-American children are given the opportunity to learn to skate. On my free time I love to hike, bake, and go on adventures.
